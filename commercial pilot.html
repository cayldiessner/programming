What is it?

A commerical pilot is a pilot who operates an airplane for transportation of mail, passengers, or freight or for other commercial purposes.

How to become a pilot.

  1. Research Pilot Schools
  2. Take an Introductory Training Flight
  3. Apply for FAA Medical Certificate
  4. Apply for FAA Student Pilot Certificate
  5. Start Flight Training Lessons
  6. Pass Private Pilot Knowledge Test
  7. Pass Private Pilot Practical Exam

Congrats! You're a pilot!

How to become a pilot video.

How much do they make?

Commercial Pilots start off earning around $50/hour, and earn about $93,000 a year.

What are some benefits?

  • You get to travel the world.
  • Flying gets you an amazing view.
  • Family and friends can get travel discounts.
  • Flexible working.
  • Meet a lot of new people.
  • Salary and health benefits.

What are the negatives?

Although there are many positives to being a pilot, there are alwasy some negatives.

  • Don't get to see your families a whole lot.
  • Miss a lot of social and emotional events.
  • Odd hours of sleeping.
  • You are alone most of the time while not flying.

Fun Facts

  • Pilots can fly a private plane almost whenever they want.
  • Pilots can write your will.
  • Pilots can read newspapers while flying, but not books.
  • Pilots and co-pilots also cannot share food under any circumstances.
  • A pilot cannot fly for more than 60 hours per week.
